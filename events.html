Energetica Exhibition

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum. It’s a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them. From solar energy powering lighting, to ‘Wind Island’ that shows how turbines are able to use and control wind to create power.

Limited exhibition between Dec 6th and Mar 26th

Kids Events

Night In The Museum

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays. Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science.

Suitable for families with children aged 7 and up. Teachers can also enquire about school trip experiences where our experts will be on hand to enlighten and inspire young minds. Contact us for more information about booking.

Contact Us

Young "Rocket Stars" Club

Our current theme is rocket science, where we explore the science behind behind rockets, satellites in space, and sending probes out into the galaxy.

Make sure to sign up for are special guest session on January 8th where Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be running a class on the science and technology that put a man on the moon.

Contact Us

Winter Holiday Club

This winter at holiday club we will take an interactive virtual tour of the solar system and beyond, as well as look at the technologies to get us into space.

To book a spot or find out more details send us a message.

Contact Us

Last Summers Holiday Club "Roboracing"

Last summers holiday club theme was "Roboracing!" where we learnt to build are own robots to race, perform special tasks. We used VR headsets and game pads to control them, as well as built a simple AI to allow our robots to navigate a track all on there own!. The kids even got to shake Rob the Robots hand.
