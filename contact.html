How to get in touch

For queries regarding exhibits, events and school visits, please fill out a query form.

For inquiries about internships, research collaborations, facilities usage and volunteering, use the email provided: CSM_Get_Involved@csm.no

Donations

Donations are very welcome and are an important way we keep this museum open and accessible to the whole community.

You can also support us by donating items of interest to the museum’s collections. If you have some item or collection that you think others would enjoy, please let us know by contacting our Collections Department by email: CSM_Collections@csm.no
